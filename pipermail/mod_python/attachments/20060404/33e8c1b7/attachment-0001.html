the &quot;SetHandler python-program&quot; directive is only necessisary in older
versions(?) of mod_python, in which case you also needed to specify a
&quot;PythonHandler&quot; directive.&nbsp; In your case, the line &quot;SetHandler
mod_python.publisher&quot; should be all that's needed.<br>
