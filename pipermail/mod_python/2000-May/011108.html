Victory. Here's what I did.

added:

AddModule mod_python.c

after the other added modules.

added this directive:

<Directory "/usr/local/apache/htdocs/ptest">
    AddHandler python-program .py
    PythonDebug
    PythonHandler test
</Directory>

and everything works fine.

thanks all
