Remember to .save() the session, then upon return, it won't be is_new()..

sess = Session.Session(req)
if sess.is_new():
  sess['foo'] = 'bar'
  sess.save()
else:
  print 'Hello %s' % sess['foo']

Hope this helps.

Peter
