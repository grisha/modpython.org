Alias seems to be the key...

<Directory "/Users/mh/mp/htdocs/xmlrpc/x1">
   AddHandler mod_python .py
   PythonHandler x1
</Directory>
Alias /x1 /Users/mh/mp/htdocs/xmlrpc/x1/.py
