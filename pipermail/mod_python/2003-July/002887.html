I think this is unnecessary. Something like this in the config will make
your handler listen on 1.1.1.1:8888 only:

Listen 2.2.2.2:80
Listen 1.1.1.1:8888

<VirtualHost 1.1.1.1:8888>

	PythonConnectionHandler blah
	...

</VirtualHost>
